Roya Naini is a composer and musician from Olympia, Washington, US and since 2010 resides in Stockholm, Sweden. She holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Music for electroacoustic composition from the Royal College of Music in Stockholm and has studied composition at the Estonian Academy of Music and Theatre. She also holds a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from the University of Washington, Seattle and has a professional background in software engineering.

Roya's compositional practice is rooted in visual structures, drawn particularly to the points at which such systems begin to tear. This has led to working closely with spectral material and the analysis of microtonal nuances in musical articulations, believing they contain meaningful symbols of human presence. In keeping with the root of her practice, her compositions often include elements borrowed from visual art such as extreme contrasts and material traces, employing these through spectralist and classical counterpoint techniques, along with the usage of modular synthesis and traditional/experimental acoustic instruments.

As a music artist and producer, Roya has released five albums of electronic pop, and played extensively live in Sweden and North America.
